### Familienheim (§13 ErbStG)
- **Spouse**: tax-free, unlimited, 10-yr continued occupancy
- **Children**: tax-free up to **200m²**, move in within 6 months, occupy 10 yrs
- On €10M/400m² villa: ~€1.1–1.4M tax with Familienheim; ~€2.7–2.9M without
