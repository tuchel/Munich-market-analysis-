# Starnbergersee Communities — Research Dossier

Source: deep-research agent, retrieved 2026-04-23.
Data vintage: Q1 2026; Bodenrichtwerte 2024 stand; listing benchmarks 2024–2025.

## Prestige Ranking (2026)
1. **Berg** (Leoni/Kempfenhausen/Assenbuch) — Wittelsbach halo, Siemens/BMW/Allianz wealth; ~€38M Leoni estate reported (SZ 2024).
2. **Feldafing** (Garatshausen/Wieling) — Wittelsbach, Krupp, Golfclub, Roseninsel view.
3. **Tutzing** (Oberzeismering/Kampberg) — Kurort, Evangelische Akademie, literary history.
4. **Pöcking** (Possenhofen/Niederpöcking) — Sisi heritage, schools, most liveable prestige option.
5. **Starnberg** (Söcking/Hadorf) — Kreisstadt, MIS catchment, S-Bahn terminus.
6. **Bernried** — Buchheim Museum, Kloster, old-money connoisseur.
7. **Münsing** (Ammerland/Ambach) — east-shore artist mile.
8. **Seeshaupt** — southern tip, Alpenblick, Osterseen.

## Per-community key figures (2025)

### Starnberg (Kreisstadt)
- Pop 23,600; SFH €10,500/m²; ETW €9,800/m²; 5-yr +28%
- Luxury P90: €14–17k; BRW lakefront up to €22k/m²
- Lakefront villa €12–22M; 2nd-row €5–9M; hillside €3.5–8M
- GewSt 310%, Grundsteuer B 310%
- Commute S6→Marienplatz 28–32 min; A95 25–40 min
- Schools: Gymnasium Starnberg, Viscardi-Gymnasium, **MIS** (buyer draw)
- Infrastructure: Klinikum Starnberg, SYC/MRSV/BYC yacht clubs
- Notable: Percha villa ~€14M (Merkur 2023); Söcking €9.8M (E&V 2024)
- Outlook: **Neutral-bullish**

### Berg (north-east)
- Pop 7,900; SFH €13,800/m²; ETW €11,500/m²; 5-yr +32%
- Luxury P90: €18–28k; BRW lakefront Leoni €28–35k/m²
- Lakefront villa €15–35M+; 2nd-row €7–14M; hillside €3.5–8M
- GewSt 280% (one of Bavaria's lowest)
- Commute: no S-Bahn, nearest Starnberg, 35–45 min total
- No Gymnasium (use Starnberg/Tutzing)
- Notable: Leoni estate ~€38M (SZ 2024); Kempfenhausen €22M (HB 2023)
- Buyer: industrial heirs, tech founders, international UHNW
- Outlook: **Bullish** — tightest supply + lowest Hebesatz

### Pöcking (incl. Possenhofen)
