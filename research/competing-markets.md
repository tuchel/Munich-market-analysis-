## Positioning synthesis
Starnberg is the **only German prime lake combining daily-commute primary-residence viability with old-money cachet**. Tegernsee beats on trophy + international brand but commute disqualifies primary; Ammersee is the 25–35% discount twin; Chiemsee too far; Zürichsee only wins with Pauschal relocation; Como only wins with flat-tax relocation + climate preference.
